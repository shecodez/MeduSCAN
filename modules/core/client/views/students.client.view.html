Go to Student Dashboard

Instructions


Step 1

Sigh up/Sign in and go to the [manage account] link under the profile image in the top navigation bar.

Step 2

Once you have the course join ID from your instructor, click the [request join] button.

Step 3

Enter the 24 digit course ID. If the course that pops up is correct click [yes, request join]. Once your instructor view the request he/she will acknowledge your request to join the course. Your request status can be tracked by going back to the [manage account] link.

Step 4

Once the request status says approved you can add the course to "My courses". Since the MeduSCAN web app is in beta test mode a beta tester key will be applied to your course. Once you've added the course to your account an admin will change your role to student.

Step 5

As a student you will be able to access your student dashboard via the [students] link on the home page where you can lookup patient EMARs listed under the course you've selected.
